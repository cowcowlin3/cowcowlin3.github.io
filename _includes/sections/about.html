Shirre Lab Corp. is a privately-held materials technology company specializing in fluoro-based dielectric materials such as Flexible Copper Clad Laminates.

We are one of only a handful of teams worldwide to possess the combination of skills required to succeed in bringing these products to market.

OUR PEOPLE

Shirre has assembled a remarkable mix of skills from both a commercial and a technical perspective. On the technical front, Shirre has a deep array of technical specialities. On the commercial front, members of Shirre have proven success in such diverse technical markets as digital video, low-power wireless gigabit Ethernet and powerline communications.
